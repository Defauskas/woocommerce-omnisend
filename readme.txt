== Description ==

Join more than 40,000 ecommerce marketers that have trusted their [email and marketing automation](https://www.omnisend.com/) to Omnisend. Our platform provides a complete solution for generating more sales and connecting your ecommerce business with potential and existing customers across multiple customer journey touchpoints and channels.

[Omnisend](https://www.omnisend.com/) provides you the tools to move your first-time visitors towards making a purchase. Streamline your sales funnel with signup forms, one-off marketing campaigns and personalized automation through different channels - all in one single platform.

After connecting your Woocommerce store to Omnisend, this plugin will automatically sync your customers, orders, carts and products with your Omnisend account, making it easy to segment your customers, send campaigns, automated personalized emails and use other smart Omnisend features.

= Installation instructions =
Here are simple few steps how you can start selling more with Omnisend:
1. Create your Omnisend account [here](https://app.omnisend.com/). Feel free to try out all the paid features for 14 days during our Free Trial
2. Download the Omnisend plugin
3. Install the Omnisend plugin and follow the instructions to set it up

= What are the technical requirements for the plugin installation? =
*   PHP 5.6+
*   WordPress 4.0.1+
*   WooCommerce plugin 2.2+
*   Omnisend account
